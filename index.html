Our Story

The first time I met Sarah was over email. I was on Thanksgiving break and she was busy starting her new job. Of course it happened to be at the same place that I work and so Sarah was already busy sending emails to people she hadn't met telling them they needed to finish such-and-such at work - I happened to be one of those people. Those of you that know Sarah will likely agree that she is one of the hardest workers you'll ever meet. But of course under all of that toughness she is also the most sincere, caring and beautiful people I've ever met.

So given Sarah's nature and fun-loving spirit it wasn't long before I was spending every hour I could with her. This lead to us both being at the office constantly followed by trips for late night dinners and catching games at Dave and Busters. Now through this whole time I'm thinking she is totally into me. I mean come-on, spending that much time together!? That had to be a sign right? Well she's going to tell you that she had know idea I liked her - even though I still don't believe that :)

Eventually she decided to give me a chance. She says it like this, "Well you grew on me...kinda like a fungus". So romantic! But given the months of effort it was the best thing I'd ever heard. A few years later we found ourselves on top of a small hilltop village in Italy named San Gimignano and a few hours after that she finally completely gave in. I will probably always give her a hard time about how hard it was for her to finally date me (and that she's a Niners fan), but I know the rest of our lives will be filled with laughter and love. I'm so happy I found my soulmate.

From BFF...

I met Bryce at work, at G2. When I joined the company, I was told I’d be working on a big project with the programmer, Bryce, who would eventually be reporting to me. Fortunately for us, the latter did not happen =). I didn’t meet Bryce until a week or so after I started since he was on vacation. But when we met, we totally clicked and hit it off. We got along great and worked well together. Over the next few of months, I got to know Bryce really well as we spent a LOT of time working late nights and weekends. I thought of Bryce as my BFF at the office. He was the person I would go to for advice or if I needed to vent. And whether it was grabbing coffee, food, or watching the games, we had a lot of fun just hanging out together.

To BF...

Bryce and I were good friends for about a year before we started dating. But, if you ask Bryce, he would tell you that we started dating much earlier. To this day, he still tries to convince me (and others) that our 1st date was at Dave & Busters only a few months after we met. We went to happy hour together to watch a UCLA vs Cal basketball game. What he fails to mention is that other people were invited too but bailed last minute so it happened to just be us.

At first, I only thought of Bryce as a friend. But the more we hung out together the more he grew on me (like a fungus). We had great chemistry but I was hesitant to date Bryce because we were friends and colleagues. But I guess it was only a matter of time before I could no longer resist the Montanian charm and Bryce’s awesome dance moves (For those of you who have not seen it, Bryce’s hips don’t lie). So after several months of hanging out, I decided to give Bryce a chance which ended up being one of the best decisions I have ever made. And that’s how the BFF became the BF. <3

To F...

For those of you who know Bryce, you know that he is an amazing and wonderful guy. He’s smart, talented, funny, charming, down to earth, and fun loving. But what you may not know is that Bryce is also a hopeless romantic. He’s full of wonderful surprises and never ceases to amaze me. And the biggest and best surprise of all happened last September when we took a trip to Italy that would forever change our lives. We were in a small town in Tuscany called San Gimignano when Bryce got down on one knee and asked me to marry him. Although it took me a moment to comprehend what was going on and I may have blurted out unknowingly… Are you serious??. There was no doubt in my mind what my answer would be. It would always be YES!

When I look back at all the time we spent together, I can’t help but think how lucky I am to be marrying my best friend. Bryce is my perfect mate and my ultimate fungai (fun guy). And I can’t wait to spend the rest of my life with him.
